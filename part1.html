Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • bread
  • oil

Recipe

  1. Combine sugar and water in a pan and bring to a boil to create the syrup. Remove from heat, add cardamom, and set aside to cool. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, paneer, sooji, Nestlé MILKMAID, baking powder, and baking soda. Mix gently to form a soft dough, avoiding over-kneading.
  2. Divide the dough into 30-35 portions and gently shape them into round balls. Heat oil on low flame and fry the balls until they turn golden brown.
  3. Transfer the fried Gulab Jamuns into the cooled sugar syrup. Once all the Gulab Jamuns are added, bring the syrup to a boil again briefly, then remove from heat
  4. Enjoy your homemade Gulab Jamun warm, garnished with your favourite toppings.
