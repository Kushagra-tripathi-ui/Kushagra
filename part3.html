FRENCH-FRIES

Ingredients

  • POTATO
  • OIL
  • SALT

Recipe

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into evenly sized strips. Soak them in cold water for 30 minutes to remove excess starch, then pat them dry with a clean towel.
  2. Heat oil in a deep pan to 325째F (160째C). Fry the potato strips in small batches for 4-5 minutes until soft but not browned. Remove and let them cool.

BURGER

Ingredients

  • BREAD
  • VEGIES
  • KETCHUPS

Recipe

  1. Mix ground beef (or your preferred meat/veggie alternative) with salt, pepper, and optional seasonings like garlic powder or paprika. Shape into evenly sized patties.
  2. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook patties for 3-4 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. Add cheese during the last minute if desired.
  3. Toast burger buns lightly. Layer the bottom bun with lettuce, a cooked patty, cheese, tomato slices, onion, and any sauces like ketchup, mayo, or mustard.
  4. Top with the other half of the bun. Serve immediately with sides like fries or salad for a complete meal.
